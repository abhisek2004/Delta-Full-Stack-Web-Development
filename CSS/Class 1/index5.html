G20

The G20, or Group of Twenty, is a prominent international forum consisting of 19 of the world's largest economies and the European Union. Key topics related to the G20 include global economic stability, trade policies, financial regulations, and addressing economic inequalities. Climate change and sustainable development are also central themes, as G20 nations collaborate on environmental initiatives and sustainable economic growth. Additionally, the G20 plays a crucial role in coordinating responses to global crises, such as the COVID-19 pandemic. Geopolitical issues, like international security and conflicts, often find their way into G20 discussions, highlighting its importance in shaping global governance and cooperation.
